                      son of Orseus, of the village of Narmouthis in the division of Polemo. ((6)) If I receive

                      monthly instalments from Sebastus to Caesareus, if you consent to the concession.
              ((22)) Sanesneus, aged 60, having a scar on the left knee. ((23)) I, Castor, scribe of the nome,
                      have drawn up this deed, since Sanesneus stated that he was unable to write.
