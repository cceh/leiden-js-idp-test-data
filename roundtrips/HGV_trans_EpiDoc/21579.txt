              Chaeremon, secretary for the public records, to Isidorus, agent at Episemou, greeting. Measure out to Apollonius and Herminus, collectors of annona, for the fourth period of six months on behalf of Ammonion six and a half artabae of lentils by the tenth measure,total 6 1/2 art., and take a receipt. I pray for your health. The sixth year of our lord Probus Augustus, Choiak 3.
