              To ..., strategus of the Oxyrhynchite nome, from Aurelius Heraclides also called Dionysius, senator of Oxyrhynchus, decemprimus of the middle toparchy. Detailed list of wheat entered on the books as measured and paid through me from the produce of the present 6th year of the Marci Julii Philippi Caesars the lords Augusti, as follows. At the granary of Tanais the Marci Aurelii, heirs of Taapollos also called Megiste, ..; 1st, paid by Heraclides also called Serapion for city dues at Tanaïs 20 artabae; 1st, the same for deposit 20 art.; total the aforesaid. Panetbeus, priest, .. art.; 4th, paid by Ko... son of ... for village dues at Tanaïs 4 art.; 1st Aurelius Achilles, Roman knight, for deposit 16 art.
