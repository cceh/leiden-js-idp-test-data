         <T=.en<=
            ((1))To [A]mmonius Paternus, 
            <centurio~la=hekatontarches>,
            (((2)))from Akiaris, son of Esoueris, and Onnophris, son of Panephremmis, residing 
            in the hamlet of <:Ma[rempi]s|BGU:BL I 46|:>, near Herakleia, [Themist]os 
            division.
            (((7)))On the night before the 21st of the present month [Pa]yni, certain persons, 
            whom we do not know, came to the private areas of land, which we farm, near the village of 
            Boubastos. And they carried off our vessels of vegetable-seed to another drying floor, no fewer 
            than twelve vessels. ((15))Therefore we submit (this petition) and request that the 
            necessary enquiry be made by those responsible for it, and that we are allowed to take away 
            what was stolen from us and that we may be helped by you. ((21))Farewell.
            (((22)))Akiaris about 30 years old with a scar on his left brow.
            (((23)))Onnophris, about 25 years old, with a scar on the middle finger of 
            his right hand. 
            (((25)))Year 1 of Imperator Caesar Gaius Pesce[nn]ius Niger Justus Augustus, 
            Payni 23.
