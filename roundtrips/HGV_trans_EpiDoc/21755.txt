              /*Translation: from P.Oxy. 8*/
              I, Macarius, assistant at the village of Serapion son of Chaeremon, have received and been paid in full on account of the public dues upon the land of Akous for the eleventh indiction, twenty and a quarter carats of gold, total 20 1/4 car. gold for dues in full, and for security (have issued) the receipt as above. Written in the month Tubi of the 11th indiction in the 239th which = the 208th year. (Signed) Through me, Jeremiah, scribe, and Phoebammon, official; agreed to by me.
