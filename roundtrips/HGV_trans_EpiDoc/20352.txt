              Proclamation of his excellency Flavius Valerius Pompeianus, praefect of Egypt. Orphans for whom no guardians have been assigned shall have guardians in accordance with their age created for them by those competent to make the appointment ...; for it will thus result that they receive proper attention, whereas at present much business concerning orphans and depending upon their guardians is delayed because the orphans are unattended by tutores or curatores. The 4th which = the 3rd year of our lords the Augusti Diocletianus and Maximianus, Phaophi... Published in Oxyrhynchus on the 247th of the same month Phaophi.
