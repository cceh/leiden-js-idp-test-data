               (Translation from: P.L.Bat. 19.25)

Year 6, Mesore 7.
X son of Harpochrates has paid for (the tax of) 5/6 artaba (per aroura) for ... for the same year: one artaba of wheat and a half = 1 ½ (artabas) of wheat.
Y sitologos [Demotic:] The receipt of the royal granary: 1 ½ (artabas) of wheat for the Southern Island, for year 6.
