               ((1))The second year of Imperator Caesar Nerva Trajanus Augustus Germanicus (month, day) at Tebtunis 
in the Polemon district of the Arsinoite nome. ((4)) Isidora, daughter of Lysimachos, Persian woman, aged about - - -, having a scar on the left brow, 
with the assistance of her son, Kronios, son of Lysanias, aged about 28 years, having a scar on the left of the neck, and Kronios himself, 
both of them being mutual security for payment, acknowledge to ((10)) Lysimachos, son of Didymos, son of Didymos, aged about 38 years, 
having a scar on the right shin, that they have received from Lysimachos forthwith from hand to hand out of his house 
a loan of a capital sum of 112 silver drachmae; ((15)) and the acknowledging persons, Isidora and Kronion, are bound to repay to 
Lysimachos the 112 silver drachmae and the interests in the month Epeiph of the present second year of Imperator Caesar Nerva Trajanus Augustus Germanicus.
