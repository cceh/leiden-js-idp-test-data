               ((1)) To Bolanus, strategus of the divisions of Themistes and Polemon in the Arsinoite nome.
               ((3)) I, Soterichus, son of Democrates son of Apion, surnamed Marianos, assistant of the sitologi of
                      the village of Theadelphia for the produce of the 6th year, ((6)) swear by the fortune of the Emperor
                      Caesar Lucius Septimius Severus Pius Pertinax Arabicus Adiabenicus Parthicus Maximus Augustus
               ((9)) and the Emperor Caesar Marcus Aurelius Antoninus Augustus ((11)) that I will recover by the fifteenth
                      of Mecheir one thousand eight hundred and twenty silver drachmae, ((13)) being the value of 227 1/2 artabae
                      of wheat measured to us on deposit by Chariton and his partners, ((16)) collectors of the crown-tax of the
                      same village of Theadelphia, and paid in by us on account of the state cultivators of the same village.
             ((19)) If I do not recover it, I shall pay to the collector the half-portion pertaining to me, nine hundred
                      and ten silver drachmae, ((23)) without prejudice to the claim of Chariton against my partner Isas for the
                      910 drachmae of the other half; otherwise may I be liable to the consequences of the oath.
             ((26)) (2nd hand) I, Soterichus, have sworn the oath, (1st hand) and (2nd hand) I will pay as
                      aforesaid.
             ((28)) (1st hand) The 7th year of the Emperor Caesar Lucius Septimius Severus Pius Pertinax Arabicus
                      Adiabenicus Parthicus Maximus Augustus and the Emperor Caesar Marcus Aurelius Antoninus
                      Augustus, Phaophi 2.
                      /*Translation: R.A. Coles, JEA 52 (1966) 133 Nr. 2*/
            =>
         =T>
