               ((1)) To Aurelius . . . (phylarch of the city of the Oxyrhynchites?) of the present 2nd year, from
Tiberius Claudius Demetrius son of Tiberius Claudius Copreus.
(((6))) Demeas, my son by Tanechotis daughter of Petteiras from the city of the Oxyrhynchi, payer of

of a similar category as is fitting. And I swear by the fortune of our lords Valerianus and Gallienus
Caesares not to have lied.
